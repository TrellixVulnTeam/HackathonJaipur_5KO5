ABOUT US

  • THIS WEB APP IS BUILT ON THE TRACK 'TECH AND HEALTH'.
  • AS WE ALREADY KNOW THAT FOR APPOINTMENTS , WE HAVE TO CONTACT THE PARTICULAR HOSPITAL/NURSING HOME/CLINIC WHICH IS TIDEOUS AS WELL AS LEADS TO A LOT OF TIME WASTAGE IF THE OPD IS NOT AVAILABLE AT THAT PARTICULAR INSTANT.
  • THUS , THE PATIENT KEEPS ON GOING TO THE PARTICUAL MEDICAL PROFESSIONAL AND HENCE THE PATIENT FEELS DEJECTED.
  • ALSO , THERE IS A DIFFICULTY IN KEEPING THE CONTACTS OF DIIFFERENT MEDICAL PROFESSIONALS.
  • WHAT THIS WEB APP DOES IS THAT , IT CONNECTS THE PATIENTS TO THE MEDICAL PROFESSIONALS.
  • THE PATIENT ON THE COMMON PLATFORM CAN CHECK THE LIST OF ALL THE AVAILABLE NURSING HOMES/HOSPITALS/CLINICS AND CAN BOOK APPOINTMENTS WITH THEM.
  • AFTER CLICKING ON 'BOOK APPOINTMENT' , THE PATIENT IS DIRECTED TO THE PAGE WHERE HE/SHE NEEDS TO ENTER THE DETAILS OF HIM/HER.AFTER BOOKING , THERE WILL BE A POP UP MESSAGE WHICH WILL TELL THAT THE REQUEST HAS BEEN SENT TO THE PROFESSIONAL WHICH HE/SHE WANTED TO CONTACT.
  • THE MEDICAL AGENCIES NEED TO REGISTER ON THIS WEBSITE AND GENERATE A USERNAME AND PASSWORD.WHEN THEY WILL LOGIN INTO THEIR ACCOUNT , THEY WILL BE ABKE TO SEE THE REQUESTS WHICH ARE GENERATED BY THE PATIENTS.
  • THEN , THE PROFESSIONALS CAN SEND THE APPROPRIATE OPD DETAILS TO THE PATIENT CELL PHONE AND HENCE BOTH ARE HAPPY.
  • THUS IN THIS WAY , THE PATIENT SAVED TIME AS WELL AS MONEY AS HE/SHE WILL GO ONLY ON THAT DAY WHICH IS GIVEN BY THE MEDICAL PROFESSIONALS.
